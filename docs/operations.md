## Before first use

Steps a reader takes once, before operating for the first time.

## Normal procedures

Numbered steps, one action each. Write them as instructions to the reader:

1. Confirm the battery is above 50%.
2. Check the propellers for damage.
3. Power on the aircraft and wait for the status light to turn green.
