## Hazard conventions

Safety information appears throughout, in three levels:

:::{danger}
Immediate hazard which, if not avoided, **will** result in death or serious
injury.
:::

:::{warning}
Hazard which, if not avoided, **could** result in death or serious injury.
:::

:::{caution}
Hazard which, if not avoided, may result in damage to the aircraft or equipment.
:::

:::{note}
Information that is useful but not safety-related.
:::

Use them sparingly. A manual where every paragraph is a warning is a manual
where none of them are read.
